About the author

Jean-Marc Fontaine is a passionate web developer. While specialized in PHP and its industrialization, he loves to learn and has a broad knowledge of all things web.

He is currently manager of Luceo Core and Platform teams at CareerBuilder. These teams are responsible for many things from building and operating the servers to providing the technical layers the Luceo applicant tracking system is built upon by other teams, such as the APIs, SDKs, framework and libraries.

His main focus is on PHP development industrialization. He co-wrote the "Industrialisation PHP" white paper on this topic.

Jean-Marc has been involved in Open Source community, especially in PHP's, since the early 2000's. He spoke at several conferences including Forum PHP, ConFoo and PHP Tour. He's also a former President of AFUP (PHP French User Group).
